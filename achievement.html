NMIT NCC achievements

1.Institutional achievements

    NMIT has received the best institution award for consecutive three times in the entire Karnataka and Goa Directorate.
  • Best institute of the Directorate 2016-17
  • Best institute of the Directorate 2017-18
  • Best institute of the Directorate 2018-19

2.National Camps

    Cadets are continuously upholding the pride of NMIT NCC by participating and achieving in national camps.

      1)The Republic Day Camp

      Cadets of NMIT have been the prestigious part of Republic Day camps,going through rigorous selection process for 3 months had been part of Republic Day held at Rajpath,Delhi and also the Prime Minister's rally. Cadets who took part in RDC

    • CSUO Mayank Kumar Choubey was the part of 'All India Guard of Honour' 2017
    • CSUO Aman Kumar Ranjan was the best cadet of Karnatka and Goa Directorate and proud Youth Exchange Programme delegate to Russia.
    • CSUO Shreyas S Hampole was the 'All India Best Cadet' of 2018 and took part in Prime Minister's rally and was the proud part of Youth Exchange Programme to Kazakhstan.
    • SUO Spandana Suresh took part in various cultural activities held as the part of Republic Day camp,was the proud part of PM's rally and was the youth delegate to Maldives.
    • Cadet Kevin D'cruz was the part of Cultural activities held as the part of Republic Day celebration and proud part of PM's rally.
    • JUO Tejas Alva was the best cadet of Karnataka and Goa Directorate and represented the Direvtorate in National level and was the proud part of PM's rally.

      2)The Thal Sainik Camp

      Cadets are NMIT have been the proud part of the Directorate to represent it at National level and immensely increase the pride of NMIT NCC.Cadets who were the part of Thal sainik Camp

    • JUO Prateek Mehta has been the proud part of Thal Sainik Camp and represented Karnataka and Goa Directorate at Delhi.
    • JUO Manasa has been the proud part of Thal Sainik Camp and took pride jn representing directorate at Delhi.
    • CSUO Shruti Khandu has taken immense pride in representing the Directorate at Delhi in map reading and tent pitching and won medal in Inter Group Competitions.
    • JUO Soni A was tbe part of Inter Directorate Shooting Competition(IDSC) and won medal in Inter Group Competitions at Directorate level.

      3) Advanced Leadership Camp

      After selections in basic leadership camps cadets are selected for the Advanced Leadership Camps.

    • SGT Vikash Jha was the part of Advanced leadership Camp and took pride in being honoured as the Adjudged Best Cadet of the camp.
