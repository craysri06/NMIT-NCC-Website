Drill

The motto of NCC is 'Unity and Discipline'.This unity and discipline is created by the practice of drill. The aim of drill is to inculcate a sense of discipline, improve bearing, smartness in appearance and turn out, create self confidence,  develop the quality of immediate and implicit obedience to orders/commmands. Proper execution of every command with proper posture and high spirt is needed. Also thing not to be done in drill like rolling of eyes, hopping and jumping, dragging of foot, clicking the heel.

We the cadets of the National Cadet Corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizens of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings.

Weapon Training

The basic aim of using the rifles is to remove the fear of a weapon from the hearts of young boys and girls. In NCC we have 0.22 rifle for weapon training as it has less recoil.
5.56mm INSAS Rifle and 7.62mm Self Loaded Rifle are used only for Assembling and Dismantling.
